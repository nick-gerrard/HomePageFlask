About this Site

This site was developed by Nick. It is coded exclusively in python, using Flask to serve the website.
It draws data from the NYT api, as well as the OpenWeather API.
I hope you enjoy playing around with it! Feel free to make an account (I won't email you) and check it out!
If you notice anything broken/could be better/think of a feature I should add, please let me know!
As this site is still in beta, there are a few features that are not complete as of now. Most notably, the password reset links do not do anything currently. If you forget your password, email me directly, and I will let you back in. Have fun!
